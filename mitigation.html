We believe that with thoughtful preparation, communities can reduce the damage caused by tsunamis. FEMA recommends:

  • Constructing tsunami-resistant buildings and strengthening coastal building codes.
  • Designing clear and accessible evacuation route maps.
  • Planting natural barriers like mangroves and restoring dunes to absorb wave energy.

üìò Community Involvement

Working together makes a huge difference. When governments, organizations, and individuals partner up, everyone is safer. We support:

  • Grant programs to fund local education and awareness campaigns.
  • Encouraging partnerships between public agencies and private businesses for monitoring systems.
  • Running local drills, town hall discussions, and emergency workshops.
