Mitigation Strategies

Evacuation routes are being updated and reinforced. New building codes require tsunami-resistant foundations in coastal zones.

Community Involvement

Neighborhood groups help distribute flyers, conduct drills, and share preparedness kits. Volunteers can apply for FEMA-led training every quarter.

Action Plan for Recovery

- Shelter setup: within 2 hours
- FEMA team dispatch: within 4 hours
- Budget: $4M allocated for first-response housing and supplies
- Rebuild incentives for community-based housing solutions
