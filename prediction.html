üìò Research & Evidence

Tsunami prediction relies on understanding the relationship between seismic activity and ocean displacement. FEMA integrates global data sources to stay ahead of potential events in the Pacific Ocean.

  • DART buoys detect seafloor pressure changes indicating tsunami waves.
  • USGS Earthquake Alerts track undersea quakes near Hawaii and the Pacific Rim.
  • Historical case studies like the 2011 Japan tsunami help model wave behavior.
  • Forecast models simulate tsunami paths using quake magnitude and ocean depth.

In 2025, FEMA plans to deploy a new AI-powered dashboard to analyze seismic patterns and predict risk with higher accuracy.
